Cloud Balance

Managing your money has never been this easy.

Cloud Balance is an application built to manage your cash and compute your cash flow using the bucket budgeting method. Once you get a handle on cash flow, you can start bucket budgeting and start reining in your spending.

Bucket budgeting is a simplified style of budgeting where money is divided between several “buckets” each designated for a different group of expenses. For some, this could be simpler and more helpful than cumbersome itemized budgets, custom spreadsheets and expensive finance programs. For basic money decisions, computing your monthly cash flow and using the budget bucketing method will do the job.


Bucket Budgeting
I
Income

Salary, Bonus, In most cases you want more.

S
Static

Cost you have little control over. Usually fixed.

D
Discretionary

Unique lifestyle choices you have control over.

F
Future

Money you save for future events.



The application is built to run in the cloud in Google's app engine. The technology stack includes Java, JSON, Angular JS, Javascript.
