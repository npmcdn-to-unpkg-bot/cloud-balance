Cloud Balance

Cloud Balance is an application built to manage your cash, compute your monthly cash flow and keep your data safe in the cloud to access from anywhere.

Sign in with Google View on Git Hub

Cloud balance is simple check register built around the bucket budgeting method. Bucket budgeting is a simplified style of money management where money flows into buckets, each designated for a different group of expenses. For some, this could be simpler and more helpful than cumbersome itemized budgets, custom spreadsheets and expensive finance programs. For basic money decisions and computing monthly cash flow, and getting idea where your money is going, this app will do the job.


Bucket Budgeting
I
Income

Salary, Bonus, In most cases you want more.

S
Static

Cost you have little control over. Usually fixed.

D
Discretionary

Unique lifestyle choices you have control over.

F
Future

Money you save for future events.



The application is built to run in the cloud in Google's app engine. The technology stack includes Java, JSON, Angular JS, Javascript.
